J.K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling is the internationally acclaimed author of the Harry Potter series, a global phenomenon that has sold over 500 million copies worldwide and been translated into 80 languages. Her magical stories have inspired readers of all ages and led to one of the most successful film franchises in history. Beyond Harry Potter, Rowling has written novels for adults and children under her own name and the pseudonym Robert Galbraith. She is a prominent philanthropist and advocate for numerous causes.
